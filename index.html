Who We Are

Built by hand. Designed for life.

Scott was founded on a single belief — that furniture should feel like it belongs. Not just in a room, but in a life. Every piece we build is shaped around the people who will use it.

We work with sustainably sourced hardwoods and natural finishes, combining traditional joinery with clean modern forms.
