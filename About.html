Our Story

We build furniture that means something

Scott was born in Enugu from a simple frustration — it was hard to find furniture that felt truly personal. Everything looked the same. So we started building our own, and haven't stopped since.

What Drives Us

Our values

Honesty in materials

We use real wood, real finishes, and real joinery. No shortcuts, no filler — what you see is what the piece is made of.

Built around you

Every project starts with a conversation. We don't design in a vacuum — we listen first, then build around your life.

Made to last

We measure success in decades, not seasons. If a piece isn't strong enough to outlast a trend, it isn't good enough for Scott.

The Journey

From one workshop to hundreds of homes

It started with a single bench, built for a friend's apartment in 2015. Word spread. More friends asked. Then their friends asked. Within two years, Scott had a proper workshop and a small team of craftsmen who shared the same obsession.

Today we've built furniture for over 150 homes and offices across Nigeria. The workshop is bigger. The team has grown. But the way we work hasn't changed — every piece is still built by hand, one at a time, with full attention from start to finish.
